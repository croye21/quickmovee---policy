Privacy Policy for QuickMove

Effective Date: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email, phone number, school, profile photo, ratings, and task activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide task matching, live tracking, payment processing, safety moderation, and app improvements.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We only share information with trusted services like Stripe and Firebase or if required by law.

4. Third-Party SDKs & Tracking

QuickMove does not use your personal information for tracking or advertising. Some third-party SDKs, such as Firebase or Stripe, may collect limited diagnostic or usage data to maintain app security, detect fraud, or improve performance. We do not use this data for advertising or user profiling.

5. Location Services

Location is used only during active tasks for Doers and to filter tasks nearby. You control your device permissions at any time.

6. Your Rights

You may update, delete, or request your data at any time by contacting us at support@quickmoveapp.com.

7. Data Security

We use encrypted services like Firebase and Stripe to safeguard your data.

8. Children's Privacy

QuickMove is not intended for users under 17 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be accessible via this page.
